My Personal Favorites

There are many places to eat in Saipan, but these are just a few of my favorites

  1. Korea House
  2. Shinsen
  3. Shirley's

1. Korea House

Korea House   I like Koreahouse because the food is quite cheap and with unlimited appetizers you are for sure to get the most bang for your buck

  • Unlimited Appetizers
  • Cheap place to eat when with a group
  • Lots of variety, while also having the option to get a nonspicy version of the food

2. Shinsen Sushi

Shinsen Sushi   Shinsen has one of the best sushi on Island, at least in my humble opinion. Even it's cheaper menu items like the Spicy Tuna or Spicy Salmon will have you wanting for more

  • Flavorful food for a decent price
  • The food leaves you wanting more
  • Almost little to no wait times

3. Shirley's

Shirley's   Shirley's is a staple restaurant here in Saipan. Known for their dishes that, while are quite steep in price, are worth every penny

  • Dishes that feel homemade
  • Their fried rice is to die for
  • Their Sisig
